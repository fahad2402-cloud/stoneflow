# Founder Bible

## Chapter 1 - Why StoneFlow Exists

StoneFlow exists to modernize the natural stone industry.

We begin as a brokerage business because it requires little capital and allows us to learn the market quickly.

Our goal is to connect suppliers, contractors, architects, builders, and buyers through technology.

We will validate business processes before building software.

Technology is a tool.

Customer value comes first.

AI will be used to automate repetitive work and improve decision making.

StoneFlow's long-term vision is to become the operating system of the natural stone industry.
